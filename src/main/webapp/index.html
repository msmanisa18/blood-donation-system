Why Donate Blood?

One donation can save up to three lives. Blood donation is essential for surgeries, emergencies, and more.

Eligibility

All healthy individuals aged 18-60 are eligible to donate blood. You can save lives by simply donating blood!

Donation Process

Donating blood is a simple process that takes about 30 minutes. It's safe, and our staff ensures your comfort.
